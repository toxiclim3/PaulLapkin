The Twin Grakatas are the akimbo secondary variant of the primary Grakata assault rifle. Despite its lackluster base damage, it possesses a very high critical chance and critical multiplier and extreme fire rate, making this weapon deceptively powerful. It also fires two bullets at once due to its innate multishot, but in turn, it depletes its high ammo pool alarmingly quickly. It is also Clem's weapon of choice.

The Twin Grakatas fire two bullets per shot. This means that the actual fire rate is double that of the listed fire rate.
The total credit cost of crafting the Twin Grakatas, including the blueprint and the building costs of its two constituent Grakatas, is 95,000 Credits.
Compared to the Grakata, each individual bullet will deal 4 Impact, 3.35 Puncture, and 2.65 Slash, which is less damage per bullet than the Grakata, but fires two of these at once, resulting in far more DPS. The weapon also has the advantage of a much more efficient physical damage mod as well as Mod Lethal Torrent, causing the Twin Grakatas to have a higher DPS.
Mods that affect the standalone Grakata, such as Mod Wild Frenzy and Riven Mods, cannot be equipped on the Twin Grakatas.
